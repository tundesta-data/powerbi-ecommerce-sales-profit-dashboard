# Power BI E-commerce Sales & Profit Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing e-commerce sales, profit, and loss trends over a one-year period.

## Dataset
The dataset contains transactional sales data including revenue, cost, profit, and profit margin by month.

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify loss-making months
- Understand profit margin trends
- Provide data-driven business recommendations

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel / CSV

## Key Insights
- The business recorded very low annual profit despite high revenue
- Significant losses occurred in specific months (notably May and November)
- Profit margins fluctuated and were not stable across the year

## Conclusion
The analysis highlights the need for improved cost control, pricing strategy review, and better monitoring of monthly profitability.
